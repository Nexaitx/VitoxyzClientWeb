We may collect the following types of information:

(A) Personal Information (from Patients & Families)

  • Health-related information shared for caregiving purposes
  • Payment details (processed via secure third-party gateways)
(B) Personal Information (from Nurses & Caregivers)

  • Professional qualifications, certifications, and license details

(c) Technical & Usage Data

We use the collected information to:
