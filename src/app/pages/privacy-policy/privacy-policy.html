Privacy Policy – Vitoxyz

Welcome to Vitoxyz Private Limited(“Vitoxyz”, “we”, “our”, or “us”).

We value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you access or use our website www.vitoxyz.com, mobile application, and services (collectively, “Platform”).

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

a. Personal Information :

Includes your name, gender, date of birth, contact number, email address, delivery address, health details (as voluntarily provided), and payment information.

b. Health-Related Data:

When you consult a doctor, order medicines, or request diet plans, we may collect details related to your medical history, prescriptions, or lab results — only with your explicit consent.

c. Device & Technical Data:

Includes IP address, browser type, operating system, location (if enabled), and usage patterns to enhance your browsing experience.

d. Communication Data:

When you contact our support team or communicate with healthcare professionals through chat or call features, we may collect those communications for quality monitoring and service improvement.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To process and deliver your medicine and health product orders.
  • To Facilitate online consultations, diet plans, and nurse bookings.
  • To personalize your user experience.
  • To send order updates, promotions, and health tips(you may opt-out anytime).
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulatory obligations.
  • To improve our services and protect against fraud or misuse.
3. Sharing of Information

your personal data may be shared only in these cases:

  • With licensed pharmacies, doctors, and healthcare providers for fulfilling your orders and consultations.
  • With Third-Party Service Providers(such as logistics, payment gateways, analytics tools) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • With regulatory or law enforcement authorities, if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

we never sell or rent your personal data to any third party.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal requirements. You may request deletion of your account and data by contacting us at support@vitoxyz.com.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures (SSL encryption, secure servers, limited access) to safeguard your data from unauthorized access or misuse.

6. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve website functionality and personalize content. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7.Your Rights

Under Indian data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access and correct your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for specific uses.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • File a complaint with the Data Protection Authority (once established).

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@vitoxyz.com.

8.Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be available on our website. Continued use of our platform implies your acceptance of the updated terms.
