Return & Refund Policy

Effective Date:[Insert Date]

At Vitoxyz, your satisfaction and trust are our top priorities. We ensure every product and service meets quality standards and regulatory compliance. However, if you face any issue, our transparent return and refund process ensures quick resolution.

1. Return Eligibility

Returns are accepted only under the following conditions:

  • You have received a wrong, damaged, or expired product.
  • The medicine packaging is tampered or differs from the order.
  • The request is raised within 7 days of delivery.
Non-returnable items:
  • Opened or partially used medicines.
  • Temperature-sensitive items (e.g., insulin, vaccines).
  • Health supplements and personal care items unless damaged or defective.
2. Return Process

    1. Contact our support team at support@vitoxyz.com within 7 days of receiving the order.

    2. Share order details and product images.

    3. Our team will arrange for pickup or guide you for return shipment.

    4. Once verified, replacement or refund will be processed within 5–7 business days.

3. Refund Process
  • Refunds will be made to your original payment method.
  • For Cash-on-Delivery orders, refunds will be issued via bank transfer or wallet credit.
  • Refund timelines may vary depending on your bank’s policy.
4. Service Refunds

For digital services like consultations or diet plans, refunds will be considered only if:

  • The consultation was not initiated by the assigned expert.
  • The service was charged but not delivered.

No refund will be issued once a consultation has started or a diet plan has been shared.
