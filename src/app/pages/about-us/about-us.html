We noticed a problem: Families were overpaying for care while chasing middlemen and homecare nurses did not have a clear understanding of the terms they were agreeing to, nor did they have stable demand.

Thus, we created Vitoxyz: a transparent platform to help nurses deliver the highest standard of care - directly and fairly - and to give patients and families control over the process of finding care that is reliable, consistent, and compassionate in the comfort of their home.

The Idea

In home healthcare, finding reliable nurses and caregivers has always been a challenge. Families often struggle with limited options, lack of transparency, and heavy dependence on intermediaries.

We envisioned a platform that stood for trust, accessibility, and fairness—where patients could connect directly with skilled homecare nurses, and caregivers could earn with dignity.

That’s how Vitoxyz was born.

What We Offer

Vitoxyz makes homecare simple, transparent, and reliable.

We connect families with verified, professional homecare nurses- without middlemen.

We ensure fair pricing: affordable care for patients, fair pay for nurses.

We bring personalized homecare services to your doorstep, including eldercare, post-operative support, physiotherapy, and health monitoring.

Beyond care, we’re building features like diet plans, live consultations, and wellness support- all in one platform.

The Journey So Far

From our very first users, we’ve seen the real impact of bridging the gap between families and caregivers.

We’ve helped people access quality homecare at fair and transparent prices, while enabling nurses to work with independence and dignity.

And this is just the beginning. Our journey continues - expanding across cities, adding new features, and making homecare more accessible, trusted, and human for everyone.
